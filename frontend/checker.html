Red Flags to Watch For

💰 Financial Red Flags

  • • Registration or training fees
  • • Security/refundable deposits
  • • Unrealistic salary for freshers
  • • Kit fee or ID card fee

📧 Communication Red Flags

  • • HR only on WhatsApp/Telegram
  • • Gmail/Yahoo recruiter email
  • • No formal interview process
  • • Pressure to decide quickly

🌐 Domain Red Flags

  • • Suspicious TLDs (.xyz, .tk)
  • • New domain under 90 days old
  • • No SSL certificate
  • • Misspelled company names
